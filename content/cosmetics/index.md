# Cosmetics

Cosmetics are optional ways to show your own personal flair while playing Age of Chesspires. 


## [Emotes](#/cosmetics/emotes)

[Emotes](#/cosmetics/emotes) are a way to communicate to your opponent during a match using fun, animated gifs.

## [Titles](#/cosmetics/titles)

[Titles](#/cosmetics/titles) are a way to display achievements you have earned while playing.
